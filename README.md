## The Problem

You've got hundreds of tracks in Rekordbox. Building a set means manually checking keys, matching BPMs, planning energy arcs — and hoping it all flows when you're behind the decks. That prep work kills creative momentum.

## The Solution

**AIDJSets connects directly to your Rekordbox database** and uses AI to generate optimized DJ sets. It analyzes your actual tracks — BPM, musical key, energy level — and builds a set with professional harmonic transitions, natural BPM progression, and intentional energy mapping.

No exports. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.

<br/>

## How It Works

```
1. Connect        →  AIDJSets reads your Rekordbox database directly
2. Select         →  Pick playlists, include/exclude individual tracks
3. Describe       →  Add context: genre, vibe, energy direction, crowd
4. Generate       →  AI builds your optimized set in seconds
5. Perform        →  Load the set and focus on what matters — the mix
```

## Features

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Rekordbox Integration
Reads playlists directly from Rekordbox's encrypted SQLCipher database. No TXT exports, no M3U8 files, no manual steps. Just your library, instantly accessible.

### Harmonic Mixing Intelligence
AI applies Camelot wheel principles to create seamless key transitions. Every track-to-track connection is analyzed for harmonic compatibility with difficulty ratings.

### Smart BPM Progression
Intelligent tempo flow that avoids jarring jumps. Visual BPM charts let you see the energy arc before you play a single track.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### Energy Flow Mapping
AI builds tension, creates peaks, and plans valleys — mirroring how experienced DJs read a room. Visualize the full energy progression of your set.

### 40+ Genre Intelligence
Genre-specific mixing algorithms from Deep House to Drum & Bass. Each genre gets tailored flow patterns, transition styles, and BPM ranges that match how that music is actually played.

### Multi-Playlist Mixing
Combine tracks from multiple Rekordbox playlists into one set. Cherry-pick individual tracks to include or exclude before generation.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

### Natural Language Prompting

Guide your set with plain English. Describe the vibe, the crowd, the energy curve you want:

> *"Start mellow and build up to peak-time energy. Keep transitions smooth with long blends."*

The AI works alongside structured genre selection to give you full creative control over the output.
